Atliq Presence Insights - Power BI Dashboard
Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of employee attendance, working preferences, and leave patterns at Atliq. It offers data-driven insights into daily presence, work-from-home (WFH) trends, and sick leave (SL) frequencies to help HR and management optimize workplace strategies and understand employee engagement.

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
The dashboard tracks three primary organizational metrics for the selected period:

Attendance %: 94.0% (Overall employee presence rate)

WFH %: 9.1% (Percentage of days employees worked remotely)

SL %: 0.4% (Percentage of days taken as Sick Leave)

Core Visualizations & Insights
1. Trend Analysis (Over Time)
The dashboard tracks changes in behavior across April, May, and June 2022:

% of Attendance over time: Shows a steady baseline of presence, fluctuating mostly between 81.2% and 97.4%, maintaining a healthy overall average line.

% of SL over time: Displays minor periodic spikes in sick leaves, peaking around 2.56% early in April before stabilizing.

% of WFH over time: Highlights a clear upward trajectory in remote work adoption over the quarter, rising from under 10% to a peak of 16.4% by late April.

2. Day of Week Breakdown
Analyzes employee behavior based on the days of the week:

Attendance: Highest on Mondays (96.5%) and lowest on Thursdays (90.3%).

Sick Leave: Most prevalent on Thursdays (1.30%) and virtually non-existent on Fridays.

Work From Home: Significantly higher toward the end of the week, peaking on Fridays (12.2%) and Thursdays (10.3%), while lowest on Mondays (7.0%).

3. Granular Employee Insights
Employee Performance Table: Identifies specific employee attendance rates, total present days, and individual WFH preferences (e.g., highlighting that some employees like Atq-485 work 100% remotely despite lower overall attendance).

Daily Attendance Ledger: A detailed matrix view tracking daily status per employee code (e.g., P for Present, WFH for Work From Home, HPL for Half Paid Leave).

Data Model & Measures
The project structure utilizes a centralized Final Data table supported by distinct conditional measures:

Attendance %

WFH Count & WFH %

SL Count & SL %

Interactive Features
Time Slicers: Filter dynamically by month (Apr 22, May 22, Jun 22) or via a custom date-range picker.

Cross-Filtering: Selecting an individual employee or day updates the entire dashboard view to reflect specific trends.
